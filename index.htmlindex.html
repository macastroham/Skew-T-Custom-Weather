For Skydiving (Vertical Winds)

Track the wind barbs along the right margin of the chart to observe wind shear. Rapid direction changes can indicate turbulence and impact jump drift calculations.

For Heat Stability (Convective Energy)

Look at the CAPE (Convective Available Potential Energy) values on the chart. Values above 1500 J/kg indicate high instability, suggesting strong pop-up thermal activity.

All sounding images are fetched in real-time from the NOAA Storm Prediction Center. All forecast data is sourced from the NWS API.
